There is no substitute for hardwork.


I find that the
harder I work,
the more luck
I seem to have.

You were born to win.
But tobe a winner,
you must plan to win,
prepare to win and
expect to win.

Never give up,
for that is just the
place and time that
the tide will turn.

You never will be
the person you can be
if pressure. Tension and
discipline are taken out
of your life.
